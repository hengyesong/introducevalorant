What is: VCT? The Valorant Champions Tour(VCT)is a global competitive E-sports tournament series for the cideo game Valorant organied by Riot Games. The game's developers.
Series runs multiple events throughout each season,culminating in Valorant Champions, the tip-level event of the tour.The VCT was announced in 2020, with its inaugural season taking place in 2021.
