Agents


Agents are the playable characters in VALORANT, representing an agent of the VALORANT Protocol. Each agent serves as a different class with four abilities.

Maps

Each match of VALORANT takes place on a map. Four maps were available at launch, and annually a new standard map is released.
Overall, there are currently 17 playable maps: 11 for standard play, 5 for Team Deathmatch, and one made for practice and training new players.
