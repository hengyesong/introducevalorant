Unrated and rank

The Unrated and rank are the most popular modes for players. Unrated is more relax mode than rank. Valorant has a rank system just like all competitive FPS games.Valorant's ranked system for the most part is fairly straightforward. There are 9 different ranks that you can achieve, and within the first 8 ranks, there are 3 different tiers. In ascending order, these ranks are called:
Iron
Bronze
Silver
Gold
Platinum
Diamond
Ascendant
Immortal
Radiant (Previously known as Valorant)
